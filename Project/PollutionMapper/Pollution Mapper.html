Pollution Mapper

Project Description

In polluted and potentially hazardous street environments, human safety can be at risk, making smart technology an essential solution.

The robot is designed to navigate streets and urban areas to monitor environmental conditions and collect critical data such as temperature, humidity, air quality (gas levels), sound intensity.

The primary goal of the robot is to minimize direct human exposure to environmental hazards by performing real-time monitoring in streets that may pose health or safety risks.

Robot Control System via Android Application The robot is controlled using an Android application connected via Bluetooth, allowing smooth and precise movement in all directions. The application displays real-time sensor data received from the robot, including temperature, humidity, air quality, sound.

The robot is equipped with a GPS module that continuously tracks its location. The Android application displays the robotâ€™s position on an interactive map, enabling users to monitor its movement and identify polluted or hazardous street areas accurately.

In addition to real-time monitoring, the application allows users to review previously recorded data. Historical readings are stored and can be accessed through the app, helping users analyze environmental conditions over time and compare pollution levels across different locations.
