At iHOW Fest 2024, I had the honor of leading Team ElectroMind, and together we achieved a great milestone by winning first place in the competition. We presented a fully integrated electronics project that combined innovation with practical application. Throughout the development process, we faced many challenges, but with team spirit and determination, we were able to reach our goal and prove ourselves in the midst of strong competition from other outstanding teams. This victory was the result of true teamwork, and I’m proud of every member of the team and the journey we went through together — from the initial idea, through implementation, all the way to the moment we were crowned winners.
